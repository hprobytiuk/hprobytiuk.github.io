Peace 4 Kids

This is exaclty that idea which one of LCC students shared during Red Cross event! The concept of this project was to organize events for refugees kids from Ukraine. I took organizational part and assembled a core team. During those events regular LCC Students also helped us and actively participated as volunteers in order to make kids happy.

In total:

  • 5 events were organized;
  • About 50 kids attented every event on average;
  • More than 35 students of LCC volunteered and helped us to organize events;
  • WE MADE KIDS HAPPY!!!
