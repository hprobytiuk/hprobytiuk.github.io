U·Win Protect

They produce high-quality plates for body armor and military extensions for professional use. In the first days of the full-scale war, they noticed that the Ukrainian defenders were fighting with inconvenient ammunition, or even without it. Having understood the need, they decided to launch the Ukrainian production of high-performance plate carriers and all military extensions.

Branding

I was responsible for the branding of this project. The main goal was to put into the ideology of the brand suitability for Ukraine, its desire to win, and that it applies to everyone.

Since U·Win is engaged in equipment for the protection of the military, we included the flag of Ukraine in the form of a shield in the logo, which speaks of the real protection of the defenders.

We also incorporated the idea of wordplay into the brand name. U·Win can sound like "You Win" and "U (Ukraine) Win"!
